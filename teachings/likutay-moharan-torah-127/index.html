127 - The Garments Are in the Secret of Chashmal, Which Is Guarding

The garments correspond to the mystical concept of chashmal (Ezekiel 1:27), which represents guarding and protection. Therefore, garments must always remain whole and untorn, for any tearing damages this guarding.
