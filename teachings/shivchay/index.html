Shivchay Moharan

שיבחו מוהרן

← Back to Teachings

Shivchay Moharan (Praises of Our Teacher Rabbi Nachman) is a collection of testimonials, praises, and accounts from students and followers who witnessed Rebbe Nachman's greatness firsthand. This work contains stories of miraculous events, accounts of his extraordinary wisdom, and heartfelt expressions of gratitude for his guidance and influence on countless lives.

Contents

Wisdom & Insight

Examples of Rebbe Nachman's extraordinary wisdom and knowledge

Love & Connection

Stories of Rebbe Nachman's boundless love for his followers

Final Days

Accounts of Rebbe Nachman's last days and passing
