Likutay Moharan

ליקוטי מוהרן | The Collected Teachings of Rabbi Nachman of Breslov

Likutay Moharan is the primary collection of Rebbe Nachman's Torah teachings, comprising 286 lessons in Volume 1 and 125 lessons in Volume 2. These profound discourses cover every aspect of serving Hashem — from prayer and faith to joy, humility, repentance, and the path of hitbodedut (personal prayer and self-seclusion).
