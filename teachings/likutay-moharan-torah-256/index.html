256 - The Name אתה (You) is a segula over the sea

The Name אתה (You) is a segula [propitious] over the sea, to subdue the waves (See Chayay Moharan - The Life of Our Leader Rabbi Nachman 136, and Parparos Lichuchmah), and the secret is "In the rising of its waves You (אתה) calm them" [Psalms 89:10] (see Zohar Noach 69 and in Mikdash Melech there [referring to Kabbalistic sources explaining divine names as protections]).
