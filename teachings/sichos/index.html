Sichos HaRan

שיחות הר"ן

← Back to Teachings

Sichos HaRan (Conversations of Rabbi Nachman) contains informal conversations and teachings that were recorded by Rebbe Nachman's students. Unlike the structured Torah lessons of Likutay Moharan, these conversations capture Rebbe Nachman's spontaneous wisdom, personal advice, and intimate teachings as he interacted with his followers on a daily basis.

Topics Covered

Prayer & Devotion

Personal conversations about hitbodedut (self-seclusion) and heartfelt prayer

Joy & Happiness

The importance of joy in serving Hashem and overcoming sadness

Faith & Trust

Building unwavering faith in Hashem's providence

Perseverance

Never giving up, no matter how difficult the circumstances

Hope & Redemption

The power of hope and the promise of redemption

Daily Life

Practical guidance for navigating everyday challenges

Famous Teachings from Sichos

כל העולם כולו גשר צר מאד והעיקר לא לפחד כלל

"The whole world is a very narrow bridge, and the main thing is not to fear at all."

אין שום דבר שאין בו תקווה

"There is nothing without hope."

אם אתה מאמין שאתה יכול לקלקל, תאמין שאתה יכול לתקן

"If you believe you can damage, believe you can fix."
