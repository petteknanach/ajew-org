"אֵין לְמַדִּים רְוָחָה שֶׁל עוֹלָם הַזֶּה"

The revach [profit] of this world cannot be measured, that is, what a person can profit in this world without expenses from himself, which they call oys la'ag [without cost]. Only from what the Creator, blessed be He, has prepared before him, he can extend his hand and profit very much.
